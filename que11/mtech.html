M.Tech Curriculum

M.Tech. program of the Institute is open to all Indian Nationals in the following specializations:

Computer Science and Engineering (Intake : 20 seats)

Specializations: (a) Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (b) Information Security

Electronics and Communication Engineering (Intake : 20 seats)

Specializations: (a) Communication and Signal Processing (b) VLSI and Embedded System
